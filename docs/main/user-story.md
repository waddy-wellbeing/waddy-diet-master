# BiteRight User Story

## Overview

BiteRight is a personalized nutrition app designed to help everyday users achieve their health and dietary goals through simple meal planning and logging.

---

## Primary User: Health-Conscious Individual

### Who They Are

Sarah is a 32-year-old working professional who wants to eat healthier but struggles with:
- Knowing what to eat each day
- Tracking her nutrition without spending hours on it
- Finding recipes that fit her dietary preferences
- Staying consistent with healthy eating habits

### What They Want

> "I want a simple way to know what to eat each day based on my goals, and easily log what I actually ate without it feeling like a chore."

---

## User Journey

### 1. Discovery & Signup
Sarah discovers BiteRight and signs up for a free account. She's immediately taken through a quick onboarding process.

### 2. Onboarding (5 minutes)
Sarah provides:
- **Basic Info**: Age, height, weight, activity level
- **Goals**: Lose weight, maintain, or gain muscle
- **Preferences**: Dietary restrictions (vegetarian, gluten-free, etc.), allergies, cuisine preferences
- **Lifestyle**: How much time she has to cook, meal prep preferences

### 3. Personalized Plan Generation
Based on her inputs, BiteRight generates:
- Daily calorie and macro targets
- A personalized 7-day meal plan
- Recipes tailored to her preferences and available time

### 4. Daily Usage
Each day, Sarah:
- Opens the app to see today's meals
- Views recipes with clear instructions and nutritional info
- Swaps meals she doesn't like with alternatives
- Logs what she actually ate (with quick one-tap logging for planned meals)

### 5. Progress & Adaptation
Over time:
- Sarah sees her logging history and streaks
- The app learns her preferences
- Plans adapt based on feedback and progress

## Success Metrics

- User completes onboarding in under 5 minutes
- User logs at least one meal per day
- User engages with the app 5+ days per week
- User reports satisfaction with meal recommendations

The platform is designed to be extended for trainers and nutritionists who want to:
- Create and assign meal plans to clients
- Monitor client adherence and progress
- Customize nutrition targets per client
- White-label the experience for their brand
